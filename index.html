Survival · Obsidian Entertainment

Grounded

A first- and third-person cooperative survival game set in the micro-world of a suburban backyard. Track every system in the game below — your progress is saved in this browser.

You play as one of four shrunken teens — Max, Willow, Pete, or Hoops — reduced to the size of an ant. Survive solo or with up to three other players: work through story Quests, explore the Backyard's Biomes, battle its Creatures, gather Resources, craft Tools and Armor, and build bases while hunting for food and water to stay alive.

How this works: Tick off each entry as you discover or complete it. Progress saves automatically in your browser. The bar in the top-right shows overall completion across every category. Each entry's ↗ links to its source page on the Grounded Wiki for full detail.
